Our solution
A browser based recording software that you can use wherever you go. With easy to use interface and color coded visuals. With a modern design look and simple controls that makes it easy for everyone to record and produce music.

We´ve set some boundaries tho. You only got four channels, like an old porta studio, for you nostalgic ones out there. The effects are presets based of most common use.
This doesn´t replace a whole studio, but the purpose is to make music recording more accessible.
Close up
The image to the left shows the four channels you can record to. You also have the ability to loop and cut away noise in the beginning of your recordings with the curtains, that you drag out over your desired field.

The second image shows the focused mode on a channel. This lets you record and edit the specific channel.
Close up
Lets take a glance at the effect controls in Fourtrack. Now that we have opened the effect controlls panel, you can easily manipulate the sound of your recording to further polished sounds.
